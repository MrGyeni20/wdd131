Liverpool FC Fan Website - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Liverpool Football Club Fan Hub

Description: This name reflects a website made by fans and for fans of Liverpool FC. It’s designed to showcase the club's history, players, and more while building community engagement.

Optional Domain: liverpoolfanhub.com (not required)

Site Purpose

This site is a digital fan hub for Liverpool Football Club enthusiasts. It serves to educate new fans, celebrate legendary moments and players, and offer fans a place to connect and express their passion through a form and fan content.

Scenarios

  • Who are the current Liverpool FC players and some of the all-time legends?
  • Where can I learn about Liverpool FC's history and greatest achievements?
  • How do I sign up to be part of the Liverpool FC fan community?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #d00027 – Used in headers, nav bar, form borders, and highlights.
  • Secondary Color: #ffffff – Used for page background and text contrast.
  • Accent Color: #222222 – Used for body text and detail elements.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Segoe UI – Used for all content and navigation for readability and a modern look.
  • Fallback Fonts: Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana – For consistent appearance across browsers.
